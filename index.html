Energy production of European Union countries

This visualization shows production of energy in European Union and its countries. Each energy type in this visualization is calculated as primary production of energy and it is based on production per tonne of oil equivalent. In "other" category are included natural gas liquids, additives and oxygenates (excluding biofuel portion), other hydrocarbons, peat, oil shale and oil sands, industrial waste (non-renewable) and non-renewable municipal waste.

It is divided into three parts:

  • Development of the production
  • Countries section with map
  • Data for each country

Source of data is from Eurostat.
